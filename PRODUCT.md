# SportsPulse Product Notes

## Register
SportsPulse is a product dashboard, not a marketing site.

## Users
Sports and prediction-market watchers who want one dense command center for market odds, scores, scraped news, story clusters, heat, and video context. They are comfortable scanning compact information and comparing movement quickly.

## Purpose
Help users spot what is moving in sports right now: which stories are hot, which games are live or upcoming, and which Kalshi markets are reacting.

## Personality
Terminal-native, sharp, restrained, high-signal. The interface should feel like an expert trading desk for sports intelligence: compact rows, real charts, clean hierarchy, and no bubbly consumer-card decoration.

## Anti-References
Avoid soft SaaS gradients, novelty cards, fake blank charts, oversized empty states, marketing hero sections, and mobile layouts that simply shrink desktop columns.

## Accessibility And Responsiveness
Maintain strong contrast, 44px touch targets on mobile, no horizontal page scroll, reduced-motion support, and local-time formatting for schedules.
